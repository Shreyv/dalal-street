Dalal Street General Instructions on How To Play

For best user experience, use Chrome on desktop/laptop. For mobile, landscape mode is preferable.
Know The Rules

Dalal Street market will be closed at 4:00PM on 23rd March, 2017. Further trading won't be allowed.

Each investor will get 15 Lacs of virtual money at the time of beginning. You will have to make max possible profit by trading shares.

Share prices will change based on our software.

In the end leaderboard will be displayed on website and winner will be decided based on the remaining balance in account.

Only remaining balance will be evaluated at the time of leaderboard declaration. Hence it is advisory to sell all shares before closing of market.

About Dashboard

Dashboard contains total companies with its available shares(volume).

You will only be able to buy shares of a company if volume is available and you have enough balance for same.

Dashboard content will reflect changes automatically at every 10secs. To see changes in market news you will have to refresh the page.

On the top right corner, you will get the market news and tips from assigned broker.

Clicking on company name will give you the graph having details like company sector, no. of shares you have, and price fluctuation of last hour.

Column 'high' and 'low' indicates all time high (MAXIMUM) and all time low (MINIMUM) prices respectively.

About Portfolio & Transactions

In portfolio part, you will be shown each total number of shares owned by you at that time.

Transaction part will have information regarding your last 12 transactions.

About Broker

You can assign broker to get tips regarding market.

NOTE: Broker's tips are not fully trustworthy source. So follow them at your own risk!!

You can only assign one broker at a time and you will have to face assigning charge everytime you assign or change a broker.

You will be charged respective brokerage on every transaction you do if you have a broker assigned.
